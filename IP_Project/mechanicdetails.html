Auto Mechanic duties and responsibilities

  1. Inspect vehicle engine
  2. Inspect mechanical and electrical components
  3. Diagnose issues timely and accurately
  4. Inspect vehicle computer and electronic systems
  5. Conduct regular maintenance work such as replacing fluids, lubricating parts
  6. Schedule future maintenance sessions
  7. Advise motorists on good vehicle use
  8. Offer our products and services
  9. Build relationships with cleints
  10. Act as an advisor and give useful advices for car maintenance
  11. Bill services
  12. Maintain equipment and tools in good condition
